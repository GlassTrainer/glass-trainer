Glass Trainer

Main project of Glass Trainer which includes persistence, service, controllers and angularjs client application.

View project on GitHub

Welcome to Glass Trainer Home Page.

Glass Trainer is a project that combines wearable technologies and sport training. This project includes Google Glass and Arduino sensors. There are Arduino sensors on the athlete’s clothes to measure the heart rate and acceleration.

Trainers can see these data while coaching the athletes on her/his Google Glass screen. In this way, trainer uses only Google Glass without the necessity any extra tools to collect these data. Because Google Glass is hands-free mode, trainer may observe the athletes more effectively and efficiently. At the same time, there is GPS (Global Positioning Sensor) to collect the location of the athlete. Also, collected locations of the athletes can be seen on Google Maps.

Besides, there is a website of this project. All collected data may be accessed through this website. In this way, trainers may observe the data of training session, compare the data of previous training sessions. Also, athletes may access their own data. In this project, trainers and athletes meet the new technologies in the training sessions.

The video presents Glass Trainer project can be accessed here: https://youtu.be/AVhngI5RCH0
